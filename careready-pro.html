Pre-apply checklist

What’s saved?

  • Auto-save writes to this browser’s storage as you edit (see the timestamp under the tabs).
  • Save version keeps up to three named snapshots you can reload.
  • Text export and Download backup (.json) are for your own copies—use JSON to restore or move to another device via Import.
  • Clearing site data or another browser won’t have your draft—export regularly.

Job ad paste (local only)

In Resume → Section 8 — Key Skills, paste a full job ad into the box at the bottom. Nothing is uploaded or sent to a server; matching and parsing run in your browser.

  • Match selected skills — highlights which chosen chips appear as plain text in the ad.
  • Turn on skills mentioned in ad — enables chips whose labels match text in the paste.
  • Parse fields & bullets — reads lines like Role: or Company – …, bullet lists, emails/phones/URLs (for copy). Use Cover / Email to fill drafts — always double-check before you apply.

Works best when the paste includes headings (e.g. from SEEK or similar). If a field is missing, copy it manually.
